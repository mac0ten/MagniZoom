# Code of Conduct

MagniZoom aims to be a practical, respectful project for building Android accessibility and magnification experiments.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in issues, pull requests, and discussions.
- Keep feedback focused on the code, documentation, behavior, or design being discussed.
- Assume normal prototype constraints unless a safety, privacy, or accessibility concern needs direct attention.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discriminatory language, personal attacks, or repeated hostile comments.
- Publishing private device data, screenshots, logs, or recordings without permission.
- Pressuring contributors to bypass Android privacy, capture, or permission restrictions.

## Reporting

If you see a conduct issue, open a private security/contact channel if one exists for the published repository, or contact the project maintainer directly. Reports should include the context, links, and any relevant screenshots or logs.

Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or block participation when behavior makes the project unsafe or unproductive.
